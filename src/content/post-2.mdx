# Services Page Redesign

Our services page has been revamped to make it easier for patients to find the care they need. Each service is clearly outlined with detailed descriptions and contact information.

- **Service Categories:** Clear categorization of healthcare services.
- **Detailed Information:** Comprehensive descriptions of each service.
- **Appointment Booking:** Integrated booking system for easy scheduling.
